EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

Two major features have been added to the Dealers Invoice system:

1. ✨ PER-PRODUCT GST SELECTION (5% or 18%)
   - Dealers can select different GST rates for different products
   - System calculates tax individually per product
   - Invoices display the GST% for each product line

2. 📋 NEW INVOICE METADATA FIELDS
   - Due Date (when payment is due)
   - P.O.# (purchase order reference)
   - Sent To (recipient contact person)
   - Ship To (shipping delivery address)

All features are OPTIONAL and 100% BACKWARD COMPATIBLE.

CALCULATION LOGIC CHANGES
================================================================================

GST CALCULATION:

BEFORE (Fixed 18%):
  Product Total: ₹60,000
  GST (18%): ₹10,800
  Total: ₹70,800

AFTER (Per-Product Rate):
  Product 1: ₹50,000 @ 18% = ₹9,000
  Product 2: ₹10,000 @ 5% = ₹500
  Total GST: ₹9,500
  Total: ₹69,500

LABOUR CHARGES:
  Always taxed at 18% (no change)

TOTAL CALCULATION:
  Sum(Product Subtotal) + Labour Charges + Sum(Per-Product GST) + Labour GST
  
Example:
  Product Subtotal: ₹60,000
  Labour: ₹1,000
  Product GST (18% + 5%): ₹9,500
  Labour GST (18%): ₹180
  Grand Total: ₹70,680
